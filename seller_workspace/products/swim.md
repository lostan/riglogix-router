# Product: SWIM (Shallow Water Intervention & Monitoring)

## Description
Software and methodology for real-time monitoring and intervention planning in shallow-water drilling and completion operations.

## Target Use Cases
- Shallow-water well intervention planning
- Real-time drilling monitoring on shelf operations
- Integrity assessment for aging wells in shelf environments

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Operator with active shelf / shallow-water portfolio
- Wells in production for 5+ years with intervention backlog
- Operator planning workover or plug & abandon campaigns

## Technical Fit Signals (news triggers)
- "shelf", "shallow water", "jack-up", "platform well", "workover", "intervention", "P&A", "abandonment"
- Mention of aging field redevelopment
- HSE incidents on platform wells

## Timing Signals
- Campaign start within 6 months
- Rig contract for jack-up recently awarded
- Budget approval for intervention campaign

## Disqualifiers
- Ultra-deepwater operation (>1500m)
- Greenfield exploration — no production history
- FPSO-tied tieback only
