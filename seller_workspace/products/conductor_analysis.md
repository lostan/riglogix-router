# Product: Conductor Analysis

## Description
Geotechnical and structural analysis of conductor and surface casing design for offshore wells, covering installation, fatigue, and integrity.

## Target Use Cases
- Conductor design review before spud
- Conductor installation optimization (driven vs. jetted)
- Fatigue analysis for existing conductors in aging fields
- Incident investigation after conductor failure or anomaly

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Operator planning new exploration or appraisal wells
- Operator in challenging seabed geotechnical conditions (soft clays, gas pockets)
- Operator with history of conductor-related NPT
- Operator running multi-well batch drilling programs

## Technical Fit Signals (news triggers)
- "exploration well", "appraisal well", "wildcat", "spud", "new well"
- "conductor", "surface casing", "casing design"
- Soft seabed geology references (Gulf of Mexico, West Africa, Brazil pre-salt rim)
- "batch drilling", "multi-well program"
- NPT or well integrity incident mention

## Timing Signals
- Well permit filing / DECOMMIT announcement
- Pre-FEED or FEED for new well program
- Rig contract for new well (6-12 month window)
- Operator entering new basin or frontier area

## Disqualifiers
- Re-entry of existing well (conductor already in place)
- Onshore well
- Well in late production — no drilling planned
