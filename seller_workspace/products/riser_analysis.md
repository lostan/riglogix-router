# Product: Riser Analysis

## Description
Engineering analysis of marine risers for drilling, production, and intervention operations — covering fatigue, VIV (vortex-induced vibration), slug flow, and installation load cases.

## Target Use Cases
- Drilling riser analysis for deepwater campaigns
- SCR / TTR fatigue assessment for FPSO or TLP
- VIV suppression strategy for harsh-current environments
- Riser management for aging production systems

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Operator in deepwater / ultra-deepwater (>500m)
- High-current environments (West Africa, pre-salt Brazil, Gulf of Mexico Loop Current)
- FPSO or FPS with production risers approaching design fatigue life
- Operator planning extended-reach or high-angle wells

- "deepwater", "ultra-deepwater", "pre-salt", "subsea"

- "FPSO", "FPS", "TLP", "spar"
- Harsh environment: North Sea, Brazil pre-salt, GoM

## Timing Signals
- FEED phase for new FPSO / FPS
- Riser integrity inspection findings
- Extension of field life beyond original design basis
- New drilling campaign in deepwater area

## Disqualifiers
- Shallow water (<200m)
- Onshore or land rig
- Jack-up without riser (BOP on seabed not applicable)
